Nov 1 - Nov 30
Early Action / Decision Applications Due

Some private colleges offer early action and decision, this usually means an earlier application deadline but you will receive your decision by Janurary. Early Action is often non-binding but it may be the case you can only apply to one early action school. Early Decision is binding and can only be broken due to financial reasons.

Nov 30
UC and CSU Applications Due

Applications to the all UC and CSUs are due on Nov 30th. No exceptions. SAT Scores do not have to be officially sent and received by Nov 30th. Once your application has been received, all colleges will send you a link to create a student portal. It is very important you do so since this portal is where the colleges contact you with important information about your application status.

  • UC Application
  • CSU Application
Nov 30 - Jan 30
Educational Opportunity Program Application Due

Educational Opportunity Program (EOP) is a college resource for low-icome, first generation college students. While it varies from schools, EOP can provide students their own academic counselor, registration priority, a small scholarship, and many helpful workshops.

For UCs, the application for EOP was part of the UC application itself. For CSUs, the application is linked here. Make sure you find out the deadlines for each school as they differ. Letters of recommendation and short answers are required for the application.
